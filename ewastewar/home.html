E-Waste: A Growing Crisis

Electronic waste, or e-waste, is one of the fastest-growing waste streams in the world. As technology advances and devices become obsolete faster than ever, the amount of e-waste generated is skyrocketing. This poses a significant threat to our environment and health.

Key Statistics

  • 53.6 million metric tons of e-waste were generated worldwide in 2019.
  • This number is expected to reach 74.7 million metric tons by 2030.
  • Only 17.4% of e-waste is recycled.
